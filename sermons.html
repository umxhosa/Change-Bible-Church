Sermon Ministry

For the word of God is alive and active. Sharper than any double-edged sword - Hebrews 4:12

At Change Bible Church, we believe in the transformative power of God's Word. Our sermon ministry is dedicated to delivering biblical truth with clarity, relevance, and passion. Through expository preaching, topical series, and Spirit-led messages, we equip believers to grow in their faith and apply Scripture to their daily lives.
